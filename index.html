TikTok Integration

FitStudioSocial connects to your TikTok account through TikTok's official API, ensuring secure and compliant content publishing.

  • Secure OAuth Authorization — Connect via TikTok Login Kit. Your TikTok credentials are never stored by FitStudioSocial.
  • Content Approval — All content is reviewed and approved by you before publishing to TikTok.
  • Rate Limit Compliance — Built-in safeguards respect TikTok's API posting limits.
  • Community Guidelines — Content generation is guided to comply with TikTok's Community Guidelines.
  • Revoke Anytime — Disconnect your TikTok account at any time. All OAuth tokens are immediately deleted.

How TikTok Connection Works

1

Click "Connect TikTok" in your FitStudioSocial settings

2

Authorize via TikTok — you're redirected to TikTok's official login page

3

Grant permissions — review and approve the specific permissions FitStudioSocial requests

4

Start publishing — generate and publish fitness content to TikTok from your dashboard
