Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 25, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 25, 2026

FitStudioSocial ("we", "us", "our", or the "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered social media management platform, website, web application, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Name — to personalize your account
  • Email address — for authentication, communications, and account recovery
  • Password — stored in hashed form; we never store plaintext passwords
  • Workspace/organization name — for multi-tenant workspace management

1.2 Social Media Account Data

When you connect third-party social media accounts to FitStudioSocial, we collect data specific to each platform:

1.2.2 Other Platform Data

When you connect Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, or other platforms, we collect similar categories of data: profile identifiers, authentication tokens, and publishing metadata specific to each platform's API.

1.3 Brand Voice and Content Data

To provide personalized AI content generation, we collect:

  • Brand voice parameters — tone, style, values, target audience descriptions you provide
  • Content history — records of content pieces generated and published through the Service
  • Content preferences — platform preferences, hashtag preferences, scheduling preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Delivery

  • Content Generation — Your brand voice data, content preferences, and account information are processed by AI language models (Anthropic's Claude) to generate personalized content suggestions, captions, hashtags, and posting strategies
  • Image Generation — Content prompts are processed by our image generation system to create visual content for your posts
  • Content Publishing — OAuth tokens are used to authenticate with TikTok and other platforms to publish approved content on your behalf
  • Scheduling — Analytics and engagement data are processed to recommend optimal posting times
  • Analytics — We process engagement data to provide insights about your content performance

2.2 AI Processing Disclosure

Important: When generating content suggestions, your brand voice data and content inputs are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI language model for processing. This means:

  • Your brand voice, tone preferences, and content prompts are transmitted to Anthropic's API
  • Anthropic processes this data solely to generate responses and does not use it to train their models (per Anthropic's API data usage policy)
  • Generated content is returned to FitStudioSocial and stored in your workspace
  • We do not send your OAuth tokens, passwords, or platform authentication data to AI models

2.3 Account Management

  • User authentication and authorization
  • Multi-tenant workspace isolation
  • Sending service-related communications (security alerts, feature updates)

2.4 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve the Service
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Developing new features

4.2 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by:

  • Deleting your account through the workspace settings page
  • Emailing us at support@fitstudiosocial.com with a deletion request

Upon receiving a deletion request or account deletion:

  • All OAuth tokens for connected platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn) are immediately and permanently deleted
  • All brand voice data is permanently deleted
  • All content history and generated images are permanently deleted
  • Account information is retained for 30 days for account recovery purposes, then permanently deleted
  • Content already published to third-party platforms is not recalled (this is managed by those platforms)

4.3 TikTok-Specific Data Deletion

In compliance with TikTok's Platform Terms, when you revoke FitStudioSocial's access to your TikTok account (either through FitStudioSocial or through TikTok's settings):

  • Your TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token are immediately and permanently deleted from our systems
  • Your TikTok profile information (display name, Open ID, Union ID) is deleted within 24 hours
  • No TikTok-sourced data is retained after disconnection
  • Content posting history records are anonymized (TikTok account identifiers are removed)

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit — All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS)
  • Encryption at Rest — Database contents and stored files are encrypted at rest
  • Multi-Tenant Isolation — Each workspace (tenant) is isolated at the database query layer. Every database table includes a tenant identifier, and all queries are scoped to prevent cross-tenant data access
  • Authentication Security — Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. JWT-based authentication with role-based access control protects API endpoints
  • Token Security — OAuth tokens for connected platforms are stored securely and are only accessed by authenticated, authorized backend processes
  • Rate Limiting — API rate limiting protects against abuse and unauthorized access attempts
  • Infrastructure Security — Our infrastructure uses containerized services with restricted network access
