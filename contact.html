About FitStudioSocial

FitStudioSocial is an AI-powered social media management platform designed for fitness studios and businesses. Our multi-agent AI system handles the entire content lifecycle — from strategy and copywriting to image generation, scheduling, and publishing across TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn.

We believe AI should empower studio owners, not replace them. FitStudioSocial generates content suggestions that maintain your unique brand voice, and you always have final approval before anything is published to your social media accounts.

FitStudioSocial is an independent product and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by TikTok, ByteDance, Meta, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, or any of their subsidiaries or affiliates.
